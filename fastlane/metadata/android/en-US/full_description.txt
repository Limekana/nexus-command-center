Nexus Command Center is the hub of the Limecore suite — a personal life dashboard that turns the things you already track into one clear signal.

It pulls five domains into a single Life Score:

* Finance — budgets, accounts, net worth, portfolio and market insights
* Fitness — workouts from LimeLog
* Studies — sessions from StudyDesk
* Work — a quick daily self-assessment
* Habits — a streak-aware habit tracker

Choose a Life Profile (Student, Professional or Custom) to weight the domains your way, set goals, and watch the trend week over week.

Local-first with Dexie storage; optional cloud sync over Supabase, account-gated. Google sign-in uses a plain web OAuth flow — no Google Play Services, no Firebase. Built to be open and F-Droid friendly.
